Eva Sophia Shimanski

Eva Sophia is a Junior at the University of Puget Sound (UPS). She is majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Spanish and Global Developmental studies (GDS). Prior to moving to the Pacific Northwest Eva lived in Washington DC and Evergreen, Colorado. Eva was born in Colorado and attended the Montessori School of Evergreen for Toddler through 3rd grade. Her father, Charley, accepted a job at the American Red Cross in Washington DC in 2009. Eva then moved with her family to Washington DC. She attended Aidan Montessori School for 4th through 6th grade. For 7th grade, Eva transferred to Alice Deal Middle School, her neighborhood International Bacaloriet (IB) Middle School. Right before High School, Eva's family made the decision to move back to Colorado and Eva attended Jefferson County Open School (JCOS) for High School. JCOS is an out-of-the-box charter school aimed at educating the entire student through experiential education.

After graduating, Eva attended the University of Puget Sound in Tacoma, Washington. Outside of academics, Eva is the Vice President of the Climbing Club and treasurer for the Women's Ultimate Frisbee Team. She also likes to get out and backcountry ski in the winter!
