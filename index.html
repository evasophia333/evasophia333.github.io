Eva Sophia Shimanski

Eva Sophia was born in Colorado and attended the Montessori School of Evergreen for Toddler through Third Grade. Eva's family moved to Washington DC in 2009 after her father accepted a job at the American Red Cross. She attended Aidan Montessori School for 4th through 6th grade. For 7th grade, Eva transferred to Alice Deal Middle School, her neighborhood International Bacaloriet (IB) Middle School. Right before High School, Eva's family made the decision to move back to Colorado and Eva attended Jefferson County Open School (JCOS) for High School. JCOS is an out-of-the-box charter school aimed at educating the entire student through experiential education.

Eva now attends the University of Puget Sound in Tacoma, Washington. She is a Junior at the University of Puget Sound (UPS), majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Spanish and Global Developmental studies (GDS).Outside of academics, Eva is the Vice President of the Climbing Club and treasurer for the Women's Ultimate Frisbee Team. She also likes to get out and backcountry ski in the winter!
